About Gliced

Gliced, founded on August 25, 2022, is more than just a coffee brand; it's a movement towards a greener, greater, and more harmonious world. Rooted in the serene landscapes of Nepal, Gliced is committed to spreading peace and joy through its organic coffee. The brand embodies a lifestyle that celebrates sustainability, positivity, and the beauty in simplicity.

Mission Statement

Gliced is committed to delivering exceptional, sustainably sourced coffee from the Nepalese hills, promoting global harmony through a premium and eco-friendly coffee experience.

Business Concept

Gliced aims to provide high-quality coffee beans sourced from the pristine hills of Nepal. The brand emphasizes sustainability, ethical practices, and a commitment to positive impact.

Brand Story: Gliced - Green, Great, and Gladly Nice

In the heartland of Nepal, where emerald hills meet the azure skies, Gliced takes root, drawing inspiration from the enchanting beauty of the Nepalese landscape. Our brand is a testament to the values we hold dear, a mission that goes beyond the confines of coffee, and a journey that begins in the verdant hills and ends with a cup of unparalleled joy.

  • Green: A Symphony of Sustainability

    • Gliced is a pledge to the earth, a commitment to sustainability that echoes through every coffee bean we cultivate. Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, our coffee plantations embrace organic practices that ensure the soil retains its vitality, the air remains pure, and the surrounding ecosystem flourishes.

  • Great: The Himalayan Legacy in Every Sip

    • Our journey begins in the great heights of the Nepalese hills, where Arabica beans thrive in the cool mountain breeze. Gliced is a celebration of this greatness, a reflection of the Himalayan legacy encapsulated in every coffee bean.

  • Gladly Nice: Spreading Joy, One Cup at a Time

    • Gliced isn't just coffee; it's a mood, an emotion, and a source of unbridled joy. From the gleaming smiles of our farmers to the satisfying sip at the end of a busy day, Gliced is gladly nice in every way. With our tagline, "Green, Great, and Gladly Nice," we invite you to indulge in the sheer delight of a coffee experience that's not just good; it's gladly nice.
